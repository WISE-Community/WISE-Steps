Reflection Note

Summary

  • A Reflection Note appears as a pop-up window, allowing you to write about your ideas while still viewing other steps in the project.
  • Click Save to save your work. Click Edit to revise your work. Click Save & Close to save and close the Reflection note in a single click.
  • You can revise your work an unlimited number of times, to create the best response possible!

Resizing the Window

  • Click any of the resize buttons (Tall, Wide, Basic, Large) at top of screen to resize the Reflection Note window.

Moving the Window

  • Click-drag the header bar at the top of the window. Reposition the Reflection Note and release the mouse.
  • Note: if the Reflection Note is moving around unexpectedly (an issue with some older browsers) try clicking once on the header bar.
