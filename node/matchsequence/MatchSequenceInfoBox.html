Instructions (Match & Sequence Assessment Step)

Match mode

  • Drag and drop each Choice into its matching target box. Click Check Answers to review the accuracy of your placements.
  • Move answers highlighted in red to a different target box. Then click Check Answers again. When all answers are highlighted in green you've completed the question!

Match & Sequence mode

  • Drag and drop each Choice into its matching target box. Then sequence the choices within each box into the most logical order. Then click Check Answers to review the accuracy of your placements.
  • Move answers highlighted in red to a different target box.
  • Move answers highlighted in yellow to a different position within their current target box.

Tips

  • All choices must be placed into a target box before you can click Check Answers.
  • Try to rearrange many incorrect (red and yellow) answers at a time between each use of Check Answers, in order to minimize the overall number of tries needed to solve the question. But if you're having trouble getting yellow answers into the correct sequence ordering, try moving just one at a time between each use of Check Answers.
