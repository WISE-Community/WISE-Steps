Instructions (Student Brainstorm)

Self-Guided Mode

  • Read the brainstorm topic, write your response in the blank box, and click Save to save your work.
  • After saving your work you can review responses from other students.
  • Depending on the project you are running, you may receive feedback about your response from the teacher.

Discussion Mode

  • Read the brainstorm topic below, then click Create Response to create a response. When you're done writing click Save to post this Response to the discussion forum.
  • You can add one or more Comments to any Response in the discussion forum. You can also comment on your own responses (for instance, in reply to a comment from a peer).
  • Flag a Response as "Helpful" if you found it particularly insightful or helpful. You can sort the screen so items with the most 'Helpful' ratings appear at the top.
